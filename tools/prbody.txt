## G5 — canvas providers + PdfRenderer rasterise harness (ADR-028 §3.2 / §7.3 layer 1)

The two thin export providers over the shared `CanvasReplayer`, plus the harness that makes the vector PDF pixel-comparable to the raster export. **G5 exit:** both providers render a page; PDF rasterises back.

### What changed
- **`ExportScale`** — `300/72` px-per-pt; `pxExtent` (ceil, ≥1, mult-before-div) is the single point→pixel rule, so raster and PDF-rasterise produce **identical** bitmap sizes (parity precondition). Decoupled CTMs (Required-fix #1): raster `scale(300/72)·contentToSheet`; PDF `contentToSheet` **unscaled** (surface is already points); `decodePxPerPt=300/72` is a **separate** `replay()` input. `previewPageToDevice` seam for the S4 Compose host (module stays Compose-free). `PaperSize` LETTER/A4.
- **`RasterPageRenderer`** — ARGB_8888 sheet → `Canvas(bitmap)` replay.
- **`PdfPageRenderer`** — `PdfDocument`, `PageInfo` in paper points, points-only CTM, `writeTo` bytes; `finishPage`+`close` in `finally`.
- **`PdfRasterizer`** — temp-file → `PdfRenderer` (own-and-guard descriptor: no double-close, no leak on ctor throw) → explicit points→px@300/72 matrix, `RENDER_MODE_FOR_PRINT` → `pxExtent` bitmap. Test harness only.
- **Tests (Robolectric NATIVE):** raster dims + fill; `%PDF-` header; rasterise-back to 300×300; raster-vs-PDF interior pixel exact-equal (cross-canvas parity seed — full multi-scale goldens are G6). `ExportScale` pure-JVM unit.
