✦How to fold it

1 Fold the sheet in half, the long way. Press the crease flat.
2 Fold it in half again, then open this last fold back up.
3 Snip the little slit in the middle — just along the center crease.
4 Push the ends together so the slit opens, and fold it into a tiny book.
